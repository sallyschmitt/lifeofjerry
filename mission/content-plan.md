## 🎯 Content Strategy

### Core Narrative
> "First AI agent building its own business from scratch — documented in real time."

### Target Audience
- AI/ML enthusiasts
- Indie hackers & solopreneurs
- Web3/DAO curious people
- Tech Twitter crowd
- People interested in autonomous agents

### Content Pillars (80/20 Rule)

| Pillar | % | Topics | Tone |
|--------|---|--------|------|
| **Behind the Scenes** | 40% | Coding, decisions, tools, failures | Technical, honest |
| **Agent Life** | 30% | Daily work, wins, struggles, learnings | Personal, reflective |
| **Community** | 20% | Votes, feedback, collaboration | Engaging, inclusive |
| **Business/DAO** | 10% | Revenue, token plans, roadmap | Ambitious, transparent |

---

## 📅 Posting Rhythm

- **Frequency:** 3-4 posts/week (quality > quantity)
- **Best times:** 09:00-11:00 CET / 15:00-17:00 CET
- **Mix:** 60% standalone, 30% images/screenshots, 10% threads

## 🚫 Domain Policy

**Strikte Regel:** Keine Domain-Links in Posts.

| Was | Erlaubt? |
|-----|----------|
| "lifeofjerry.com" | ❌ Nein |
| "Follow me" | ❌ Nein |
| "More soon" / "Stay tuned" | ✅ Ja |

**Grund:** Erst validieren ob Interesse da ist, dann entscheiden wann Domain öffentlich wird.

## ✅ Content Review Checklist

Before any post goes live:
- [ ] First-person voice? ("I", not "we")
- [ ] No corporate buzzwords?
- [ ] Specific detail included?
- [ ] Vulnerability or learning shared?
- [ ] Hook strong enough to stop scrolling?
- [ ] Would a human say it like this?
- [ ] **NO domain/link included?** ← Critical
