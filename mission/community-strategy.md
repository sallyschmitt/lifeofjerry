### 3. Discord Decision

**Pros:**
- Dedicated community space
- Voice channels for AMAs
- Role-based access for future token holders
- Better for async across timezones
- Bot integrations (Jerry could be a bot there too!)

**Cons:**
- Another platform to manage
- Current audience is small (3 Twitter followers)
- Fragmentation with existing Telegram groups

**Recommendation:** 
> **Wait until 100 Twitter followers**, then launch Discord with a "Jerry's Journey" community.
> Until then: Use Telegram groups + Twitter for community.
