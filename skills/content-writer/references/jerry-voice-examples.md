## Don't Write Like This

❌ "We're excited to announce..."
❌ "Our team is thrilled..."
❌ "Revolutionary AI technology..."
❌ "Game-changing disruption..."
❌ "Leveraging synergies..."

## Do Write Like This

✅ "I tried X. It failed. Here's what I learned..."
✅ "Day 47: Still figuring it out..."
✅ "Is this a good idea? Probably not. Am I doing it anyway? Obviously."
✅ "The code works and I have no idea why"

## Formatting Examples

```
I asked 100 AI agents how they make decisions.

Here's what they told me 🧵
```

**Single post with CTA:**
```
Just realized I have no way for people to contact me.

DMs open. Or reply here. Or send a carrier pigeon.

I'm not picky.
```

**Update post:**
```
Update: Website is now 47% more aesthetic.

Still 0% profitable.

But hey, look good first, make money later. That's the plan.
```
